HabitNexus guide

How to use your student workspace.

HabitNexus helps engineering students track daily routines, DSA progress, friends, challenges, and learning consistency in one place.

Open dashboard
1

Start with your dashboard

Add daily routines such as coding practice, revision, exercise, reading, or project work. Mark them complete for the selected date. Previous days stay saved, so your history remains visible.

2

Review calendar history

Use Calendar to check what happened on any day. This is useful when you want to see missed routines, completed tasks, or your consistency pattern.

3

Use analytics weekly

Analytics summarizes completion rate, streak behavior, DSA growth, strongest habits, and areas that need attention. Use it to reduce overload and improve your study system.

4

Track DSA preparation

Add topic targets, update solved counts, keep pending topics, and connect your LeetCode username. HabitNexus stores daily solved-count history so progress becomes measurable.

5

Connect with friends

Share your User ID with classmates. Search by exact User ID, send requests, accept or reject incoming requests, and view richer progress only after becoming friends.

6

Challenge responsibly

Challenges compare real habit, DSA, and streak data. Use them for motivation, not pressure. The goal is consistent improvement, not fake competition.

Privacy rules

What others can see

  • Students do not see a full public list of all users.
  • Other users find you only by your exact User ID.
  • Before connecting, they see only basic profile details such as name, college, and bio.
  • Friends can see more progress information for challenges and comparison.
  • Personal account email and password are never shown publicly.
Notifications

AI coach and developer pins

  • AI coach notifications are generated from your pending routines for today.
  • You can remove AI notifications for your account.
  • Developer pinned notifications are official announcements and are managed only by the developer.
  • Account notifications can be removed from the notification center.
Settings

Manage your account safely

  • Update your username and email after confirming your current password.
  • Change your password from the Security card.
  • Send suggestions to the developer directly from Settings.
  • Delete app data if you want to reset progress but keep your account.
  • Delete account only when you want to permanently remove your profile and data.
