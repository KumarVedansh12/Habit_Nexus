What is actually in the product

A focused system, not a feature wishlist.

Every capability below is connected to working routes and stored progress in HabitNexus.

01

Daily habit workspace

Create routines, complete them by day, build full-day streaks, and inspect historical details in the calendar.

02

Useful analytics

Review 14-day completion rates, consistency, strongest habits, attention areas, and DSA growth.

03

DSA preparation

Sync public LeetCode statistics, track topic targets, manage a backlog, and see daily problem growth.

04

Student network

Connect with other students, view public progress profiles, compare real metrics, and create challenges.

05

Weekly leaderboard

See a transparent score based on habit completion, new DSA solves, and full-day streaks.

06

Portable progress

Export habit and DSA history as CSV instead of locking learning data inside the application.

A simple loop

Plan, record, review, improve.

1

Set a realistic system

Add routines and DSA topic targets that fit your current semester.

2

Record honest progress

Complete daily actions and sync coding results without invented scores.

3

Review patterns

Use calendar and analytics views to find what is reliable and what needs reducing.

4

Grow with accountability

Share a public overview, connect with friends, and compare progress transparently.
